Fracas is a facial recognition automated classroom attendance system.
To use Fracas you need to follow these steps: 

If your are not registered yet ( if you are registered go to #3)

1. Click Add Face & Name to register your name and Id and to capture your face.(Put your namne inside a quotation mark like this: "Your Name")

2. Click Train Recognizer to train the recognizer.

3. Click Auto Attendance, wait for the recognizer to recognize you, it will automatically log you in the attendance log.

4. To show the attendance log, Click Attendance Log.
